About us

Virtual Lab-IIITVICD forms the backbone of all projects created and performed by the students of IIIT-VICD during their course of BTech .It provides them a platform to show up their skills to the world.Students are the life and soul of all educational enterprise. At the IIIT-VICD, Students have the unique opportunity of not just witnessing an Institution in the making but participating in the making as well. Winston Churchill once said that we shape our Institution and these in turn shape our lives. The heady experience of joining hands with the Director, the faculty and staff in deciding almost all aspects of campus life and in evolving a culture so unique to the IIIT of the Shivaliks is part of what makes our life at IIIT-VICD unique!
